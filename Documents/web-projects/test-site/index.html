Through my journey of achieving this goal, i stumbled upon a video about Coding Bootcamps. The video explained how bootcamps were the quickest, but also intense, way of entering the tech industry. I did further research on bootcamps and realized this was perfect for me. I researched the best bootcamps in my area and DigitalCrafts was at the top of the list.
